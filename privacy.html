Geo Redes - Privacy Policy

Geo Redes is an Android app that helps users check video quality and prepare videos for posting on social media platforms.

Information We Collect

The app may access video files selected by the user in order to analyze technical information such as resolution, duration, bitrate, format, and file size.

Video Files

Geo Redes does not sell user videos. Videos are used only for the functions selected by the user, such as checking quality or preparing content for social media posting.

Social Media Login

If the user connects a social media account, the app may use official authorization systems provided by platforms such as TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook.

Permissions

The app may request permissions needed to select files, analyze videos, and connect with supported social media platforms.

Third-Party Platforms

When users connect or publish to third-party platforms, those platforms may process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Security

Geo Redes is designed to use selected files only for app functionality. Users should not share private tokens, secrets, or login credentials with others.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes or when platform requirements change.

Contact

For questions about Geo Redes, contact the app developer through the official project repository.
